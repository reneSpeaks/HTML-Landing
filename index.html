About Us.

At René's Furry Friends, our mission is to find forever homes for dogs in need, while providing a safe and supportive environment for both our canine companions and adoptive families. Our dedicated team is passionate about matching the right dog with the right family, ensuring a smooth transition for all.

Why Adopt From Us?

We are commited to:
  • Providing comprehensive information about each dog's history, behavior, and special needs.
  • Offering post-adoption support and resources to help with the transition.
  • maintaining a network of foster homes and volunteers to care for dogs awaiting adoption.
  • Patnering with local shelters and rescue organizations to help reduce the number of dogs in need.
